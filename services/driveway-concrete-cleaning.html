Driveway & Concrete Cleaning

Remove oil stains, tire marks, and dirt from driveways, sidewalks, and patios.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our hot water pressure washing system blasts away years of grime from concrete surfaces. We treat stains with eco-friendly degreasers and rinse thoroughly, leaving your driveway looking like new. Suitable for asphalt, stamped concrete, and pavers.

What's included

  • Eliminates oil, grease, and tire marks
  • Restores color to faded concrete
  • Prevents slip hazards from mold
  • Increases property value

How it works

1

Inspection & Pre-Treatment

We assess the surface and apply a biodegradable degreaser to break down tough stains.

2

Hot Water Pressure Wash

Using 200°F water at optimal pressure, we clean the surface without etching or damage.

3

Rinse & Final Walkthrough

We rinse all residue and inspect the area to ensure complete satisfaction.

Common questions

Will pressure washing damage my driveway?

Not when done correctly. We adjust pressure based on surface type and use hot water to avoid etching.

How long does it take for concrete to dry?

Typically 2-4 hours depending on weather. You can walk on it immediately, but avoid parking for 24 hours.
